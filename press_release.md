### New Open Dataset Published: Database of confirmed and reported dengue cases in 31 countries across Latin America, South Asia, and Southeast Asia, with time series from 1995 to 2024. Data compiled from PAHO/WHO and national health ministries.

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Database of confirmed and reported dengue cases in 31 countries across Latin America, South Asia, and Southeast Asia, with time series from 1995 to 2024. Data compiled from PAHO/WHO and national health ministries.*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
